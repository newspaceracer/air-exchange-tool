Iconography

Icons are inline Lucide SVGs — no icon dependency. The esa-icon primitive inherits currentColor and maps to five sizes (xs 14 · sm 16 · md 20 · lg 24 · xl 28).

Sizes

xs · 14 sm · 16 md · 20 lg · 24 xl · 28

Color

Icons inherit currentColor — set color on a wrapper to tint them. These use this spoke's themed semantic roles.
